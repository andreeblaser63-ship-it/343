About KIS Edge MAX Company

Founded in London, we provide standardized, data-driven financial consulting services across global financial markets

We Are Trusted Financial Information Consultants In London

KIS Edge MAX is registered and operated at England London 60 Broadwick St, established by senior financial analysts and investment consultants with decades of experience in international capital markets. Our core business covers cross-border financial information collection, market trend research, risk assessment consulting and investment strategy customization.

We strictly follow UK financial service regulatory standards, integrate real-time global financial big data resources, and deliver objective, actionable consulting reports to thousands of corporate clients and private investors across Europe, Asia and North America every year.

Our mission is to bridge the information gap in global financial markets, providing clients with transparent, accurate and timely market insights to support their investment decision-making. We adhere to the principle of "client-centric" service, and customize solutions according to the risk appetite, investment scale and strategic objectives of different clients.

Over the past 15 years, KIS Edge MAX has built a mature service system covering more than 50 countries and regions, with a professional team of over 80 people, including CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst), FRM (Financial Risk Manager) and other senior certified professionals.
